natural forces

As probably seen through the last assignment, I really like the ability to place and color these particles on screen. So building off of last week a bit, I added in the gravity and thus bouncing aspect of these particles. Additionally I made sure that when you place a particle, the origial direction of it goes up so we can see the drama of the fall.

I played with the gravity vector, just testing random numbers until I liked how it affected the particles. I wanted it to look mostly like actual gravity but be a bit dramatized so I settled on gravity = createVector(0, 0.002). This allows the particles to bounce more times than you would expect in real life but still eventually calm down. I also like how this number allows the particles to roll around on the floor a bit after they stop bouncing too.
